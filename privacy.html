Privacy Policy

Effective: July 18, 2026

The Launch Feed ("we") operates a first-party content publishing tool ("Launch Feed Publisher") used solely to publish our own original short-form videos to our own social media accounts.

Data we collect

None from third parties. The tool authenticates only our own social media accounts via each platform's official OAuth flow and stores those access tokens locally on our own hardware. We do not collect, store, or process any data about other users, viewers, or any third party.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties. Platform tokens are used exclusively to upload our own content via official APIs.

Data retention & deletion

Access tokens are retained only while the tool is in use and can be revoked at any time from the connected platform's security settings. To contact us about privacy: kyle@etceteracompanies.com.
